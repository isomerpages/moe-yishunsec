<p>During your child’s developmental years, you may from time-to-time encounter
parenting challenges, from disobedience to temper tantrums. Some of these
challenges may intensify over time, particularly when children reach their
teens.</p>
<p>To support you in your journey towards building a confident and resilient
child, and strengthening your parent-child relationship, Yishun Secondary
School has partnered with the Ministry of Social &amp; Family Development
on the Positive Parenting Programme (Triple P). As part of the programme,
online seminars are conducted to equip parents with knowledge and skills
in managing children’s behavior during their teens.</p>
