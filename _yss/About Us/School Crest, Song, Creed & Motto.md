<h3>School Crest</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:20%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/school-crest1.png">
</div>
<p>Our School Crest is in the shape of a drop of water that symbolises the
thirst for knowledge.</p>
<p>The branches represent our 3 core values that form the guiding principles
to achieve the 3 desired outcomes for an Yishunite.</p>
<p>The progressive growth of the branches indicates the different stages
of learning our pupils have to go through before achieving their ultimate
goal.</p>
<p>The circle represents the fruits of labour that can be achieved through
dedication, determination and self-discipline.</p>

<h3>School Creed</h3>
<p>As true Yishunites,

<br>And project its spirit.
