<h3>Key Student Outcomes</h3>
<p>Girl Guides aim to provide opportunities for girls to discover and develop
their strengths, their skills and their fullest potential, to become responsible
citizens of the world.</p>

<h3>Student’s Quotes</h3>

I became more independent and confident as I had to execute my proposal to Girl Guides from another school.
