<h3>Key Student Outcomes</h3>

<h3>Highlights / Achievements</h3>
<p>Robotics continues to offer enriching learning opportunities for our YSS
Robotics students through a wide range of activities. Both our CCA training
sessions and participation in external competitions help foster camaraderie,
build resilience, and showcase the studentsâ€™ creativity and innovation
as they strive for excellence.</p>
<p>In 2025, our students participated in a training programme that introduced
them to various technology domains and essential 21st-century skillsets.</p>
<p>Secondary One students explored game creation using Scratch and embarked
on intermediate Lego Robotics projects using Spike Prime.</p>
<p>Secondary Two students advanced their learning through a more in-depth
Lego Robotics programme.</p>
<p>Secondary Three students took part in the Intel Artificial Intelligence
Programme, where they were introduced to advanced Python programming.</p>
<p>In October 2024, our students competed in the Ngee Ann Polytechnic Aerobot
Challenge, which aims to spark interest in autonomous technology among
Secondary 2 and 3 students. The competition challenges participants to
solve simulated real-world problems using drones and catapult (not robots)
in a team-based format.</p>
<p>We are proud to share that one of our teams emerged as Champions in this
prestigious challenge. Congratulations to our students on this outstanding
achievement!</p>
