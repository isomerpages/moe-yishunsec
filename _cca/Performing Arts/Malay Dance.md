<h3>Key Student Outcomes</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To learn and perform both contemporary and traditional forms of Malay
Dance</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop interest in the art of dance and performance skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop leadership capacity within students and encourage community
involvement through dance performance</p>
</li>
</ul>
