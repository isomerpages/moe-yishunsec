<h3>Key Student Outcomes</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To develop confident and professional dancers.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To appreciate Indian Dance aesthetics and mold them into excellent leaders
to demonstrate core values such as strong ethics, perseverance and providing
moral support for team members.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To expose the students to different forms of dance and inculcate the passion
for arts.</p>
</li>
</ul>
