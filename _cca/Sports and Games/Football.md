<h3>Football (Boys/Girls)</h3>
<p><strong>YSS is a School Football Academy under the Unleash The Roar programme! (<a href="https://www.unleashtheroar.com.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.unleashtheroar.com.sg</a>)</strong>
</p>
<p>In 2024, YSS will introduce a girls’ football for the incoming secondary
one girls as part of this programme. This will be a cluster CCA for all
girls from the N2 cluster.</p>

<h3>Key Student Outcomes</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To inculcate in students the values of sportsmanship and resilience</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To promote awareness of inclusion, etiquette and fair play</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To foster friendship and solidarity</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Highlights / Achievements</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>School Football Academy under the “Unleash The Roar!” programme</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Embarked on the yearly National School Games (NSG) Football Tournament
where our ‘B’ and ‘C’ Division boys gained invaluable experience</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Our ‘B’ Division boys qualified for NSG League 1 together with the top
16 schools in Singapore in 2023</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Organised Yishun Invitational Football Carnival where opportunities were
given for schools in our vicinity pitch their skills against each other.
Our boys did us proud and clinched 1st place in 2023</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participated in NorthLight Futsal Fiesta where our boys emerged as champions</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Events in 2023</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Joined School Football Academy (SFA) under Unleash the Roar! – a national
movement to raise the levels of Singapore football and rally the nation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Started Direct School Admissions (DSA)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Competed in SFA Invitational League 2023</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parent engagement session for parents of prospective Primary 6 students</p>
</li>
</ul>
