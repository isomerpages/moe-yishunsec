<h3>Announcements for Graduating Classes of 2023</h3><p>DMA will be uninstalled from your child’s/ward’s PLD on the following date as he/she is graduating from the school.</p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p><strong>4NT 2023</strong> - Date of uninstallation: <strong>30 November 2023</strong> at 12pm</p></li><li><p><strong>4E, 5NA, 4N2^ and 4N3^ 2023</strong> - Date of uninstallation: <strong>05 January 2024</strong> at 12pm</p></li><li><p><strong>4N1^, 4N2^ and 4N3^ 2023</strong> - Date of uninstallation: <strong>31 January 2024</strong> at 12pm</p></li></ul><p><em>^Uninstallation ONLY for students who are not continuing with 5NA course in 2024</em>.</p><p>After the DMA has been uninstalled, your child/ward will be able to use the PLD without the DMA. Your child’s/ward’s browsing activity will no longer be monitored and the browsing history on his/her PLD will also be deleted from the DMA system server.</p><p><strong>Actions required by your child/ward:</strong></p><p><strong>At least one day before the DMA uninstallation</strong>. Please ensure that all data on your child’s/ward’s PLD have been backed-up (e.g. saved on an external storage device or to a cloud storage) prior to the uninstallation. This will prevent any loss of important data when the DMA is uninstalled.</p><p><strong>On the day of DMA uninstallation.</strong> Please ensure that the PLD is switched off from 6:00 am to 11:00 pm. This is to ensure that the uninstallation process, which will be done remotely, can proceed smoothly.</p><p><strong>After the DMA has been uninstalled.</strong> Starting from the next day after the DMA uninstallation, you may refer to the attached document for instructions to check if the DMA has been successfully uninstalled from your child’s/ward’s PLD.</p><p><strong>Technical Support:</strong></p><p>Should your child/ward require technical assistance to perform the back-up, he/she can call the school-based helpline: <strong>6876 7129 Ext 167</strong>.</p><p>If you find that the DMA in your child’s/ward’s PLD has not been uninstalled properly, please contact the school at <strong>6876 7129.</strong></p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p><a
