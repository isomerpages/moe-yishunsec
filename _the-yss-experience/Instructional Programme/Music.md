<h3>Department Goals</h3>
<p>Nurturing creative contributors through authentic music-making experiences.
Students develop musical skills alongside essential 21st-century capabilities
including creativity, communication, and teamwork.</p>
<h3>What We Offer</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Authentic performance opportunities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Professional recording experience</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Industry artist mentorship in Music Production</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Key Programmes</h3>
<p><strong>Instrument Fundamentals</strong>
</p>
<p>Students build core skills in guitar, keyboard, and cajon through structured
lessons and performances.</p>
<p><strong>Music Technology &amp; Arrangement</strong>
</p>
<p>Students engage with playing, recording, digital music creation, and arrangement
using Digital Audio Workstations (DAWs) - professional software used for
music production and recording.</p>
<p><strong>Industry-Relevant Enrichment</strong>
</p>
<p>Students gain real-world experience with music professionals through masterclasses
and production projects.</p>
<h4><strong>Building Strong Instrumental Foundations</strong></h4>
<p>In Lower Secondary, students acquire playing skills in <strong>guitar, keyboard, and cajon</strong> through
structured, performance-based lessons. They showcase these skills in school
performances, which enable them to build confidence, demonstrate learning,
and develop personal expression.</p>

<h4><strong>Towards Real-World Music Making</strong></h4>
<p>At Secondary Two, students participate in a specially curated programme
co-developed by YSS Music teachers and industry professionals under the <strong>National Arts Council (NAC) Artist-In-School Scheme (AISS)</strong>.
Through hands-on projects in music arrangement and recording, students
gain insight into contemporary music practices and the collaborative nature
of music production.</p>

guest musicians from the industry. These sessions develop higher-order
musical skills while broadening students’ understanding of the arts sector</p>

<h3><strong>Student Achievements</strong></h3>
<p>Our students’ talent and creativity have been recognised on national platforms
such as the <strong>Singapore Youth Festival (SYF) Youth Station Project</strong>.</p>
<p>Most recently in 2025, <strong>Ahmad Tirmidzi (4IN</strong>) premiered
his original song “<em>Goodbye To This Place</em>” at <strong>SCAPE Ground Theatre</strong>.
His work will also be featured at the <strong>Principals’ Appointment &amp; Appreciation Ceremony</strong> —
a proud reflection of our students’ real-world success.</p>
