<h3>Desired Outcomes of Literature</h3>
<p><strong>We aim to develop students to be:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Empathetic and Global Thinkers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Critical Readers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Creative Meaning Makers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Convincing Communicators</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Empathetic and Global Thinkers</h3>
<p><strong>Students will learn to:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>empathise with other people’s experiences</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>be open to appreciate multiple perspectives</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>develop global awareness through the study of texts from different parts
of the world</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>reflect on their own values, perspectives and identities as they consider
the impact of their beliefs and actions on society</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Critical Readers</h3>
<p><strong>Students will learn to:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>appreciate the aesthetic value and enjoy the nuances of language, with
perceptive and questioning minds</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>read widely and independently, from a variety of genres for the appreciation
of genre conventions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>appreciate the contexts in which literary texts are written, read and
understood</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Creative Meaning Makers</h3>
<p><strong>Students will learn to:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>embrace ambiguity and uncertainty as necessary in the meaning making process</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>actively and creatively construct their understanding and interpretation
of texts</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Convincing Communicators</h3>
<p><strong>Students will learn to:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>demonstrate critical thinking when discussing and writing about texts</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>convince others of their interpretations, based on sound reasoning with
evidence</p>
</li>
</ul>
