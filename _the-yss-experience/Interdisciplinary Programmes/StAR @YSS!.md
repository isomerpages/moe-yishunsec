<p>The StAR Programme experience @Yishun Secondary School</p>
<p><strong>StAR</strong>&nbsp;(<strong>S</strong>tudents-<strong>A</strong>ll-<strong>R</strong>ise)
Programme is part of Yishun Secondary School’s (YSS)&nbsp;<strong>Project Enable</strong>&nbsp;initiatives
to support the Lower Secondary Normal stream students. A day in a week
is set aside for them to experience a&nbsp;<strong>school-based curriculum</strong>&nbsp;that
allows them to&nbsp;<strong>seek</strong>&nbsp;their interests,&nbsp;<strong>strive</strong>&nbsp;in
their passion and soar for better educational outcomes (aligned to School
Motto). This is an&nbsp;<strong>uplifting</strong>&nbsp;strategy so that
these students stay committed and connected to the school and their learning
experiences.</p>
<p>The programme-cycle commences with an&nbsp;<strong>internal scan</strong>&nbsp;to
gather information on the overall learners’ profile through teachers’ feedback,
student-surveys and focused group discussions. This is followed with an&nbsp;<strong>external scan</strong>&nbsp;to
source for partners to match students’ interests. Weaved with&nbsp;<strong>AfL and SEL Competencies</strong>,
students’ learning are measured, monitored and reviewed frequently.</p>
<p>Support from the school and&nbsp;<strong>collaborations</strong>&nbsp;with
various community partners who believe in these students are contributing
factors for the programme’s success. Through StAR, students are&nbsp;<strong>engaged purposefully</strong>&nbsp;and
they acquire&nbsp;<strong>a sense of achievement</strong>. To share this
journey with the fraternity, YSS welcomes teacher attachment and sharing
at different platforms.</p>
