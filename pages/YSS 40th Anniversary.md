<p><strong>YSS40 Homecoming</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Come Home to Celebrate 40 Years of Memories</strong>
</p>
<p>For 40 years, Yishun Secondary School has been more than just a school—it
has been a place where friendships were forged, memories were created,
and dreams began.</p>
<p>As we celebrate <strong>YSS40</strong>, we warmly invite all former students,
former teachers and former school leaders to return home and be part of
this special milestone. Whether you were a student, taught in our classrooms,
or led our school community, your contributions have shaped the YSS story,
and we would be honoured to welcome you back.</p>
<p><strong>YSS40 Homecoming</strong>
</p>

<br>🕓 <strong>4:00 pm to 6:30 pm</strong>
<br>📍 <strong>Yishun Secondary School</strong>
