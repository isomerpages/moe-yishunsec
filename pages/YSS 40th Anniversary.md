<p><strong>YSS40 Homecoming</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Come Home to Celebrate 40 Years of Memories</strong>
</p>
<p>For 40 years, Yishun Secondary School has been more than just a school—it
has been a place where friendships were forged, memories were created,
and dreams began.</p>
<p>As we celebrate <strong>YSS40</strong>, we warmly invite all Yishunites
to return home and be part of this special milestone. Whether you graduated
recently or many years ago, we'd love to welcome you back to reconnect
with familiar faces, relive cherished memories, and create new ones together.</p>
<p>Look forward to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>🤝 Reconnecting with your former teachers and school friends</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>⚽🏀 Friendly football and basketball matches</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>🍭 A taste of nostalgia with old-school snacks</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>🚶 A YSS Heritage Walk to revisit familiar places and discover how the
school has grown</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>📸 Fun photo booths to capture reunion moments and take home lasting memories</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>🎉 Plenty of opportunities to reminisce, reconnect, and celebrate the
YSS spirit</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Every classroom, corridor and corner holds a story. Every Yishunite has
a place in our story.</p>
<p>Come back to where it all began, revisit the places that shaped your school
days, and celebrate this milestone with fellow Yishunites across the generations.</p>
