<p><strong>Consultation</strong>
</p>
<p>To support students who require help in their learning beyond curriculum
time, consultation is conducted after school to bridge the gap. This allows
students to consult teachers to reinforce their knowledge and understanding.
A school-wide consultation schedule is prepared to ensure students are
able to participate in consultation for all subjects.</p>
