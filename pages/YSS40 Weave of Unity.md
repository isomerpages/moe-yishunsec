<h3><strong>YSS40 Weave of Unity Sets Singapore Book of Records</strong></h3>

<p>As part of the YSS40 celebrations, Yishun Secondary School marked a significant
milestone on 5 March 2026 with the Weave of Unity event. Students, teachers
and staff came together in a meaningful activity that symbolised the strength
of the school community — each strand contributing to a stronger whole.</p>
<p>During the event, the YSS community successfully set a Singapore Book
of Records achievement for the Most Number of People Weaving Paracord Keychains
at the Same Time, with 1,151 participants. This accomplishment has been
officially recognised on the Singapore Book of Records website, and can
be viewed at:</p>
<p><a href="https://singaporerecords.com/most-number-of-people-weaving-paracord-keychains-together/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://singaporerecords.com/most-number-of-people-weaving-paracord-keychains-together/</a>
</p>
<p>The paracord keychain serves as a tangible representation of the school’s
40-year journey and achievements. Each knot in the weave symbolises significant
milestones, successful cohorts and notable accomplishments in the school’s
history. The vibrant colours of the paracord reflect the diverse talents
and successes of students, staff and alumni. The national recognition of
this achievement not only commemorates the scale of participation, but
also affirms the unity, shared purpose and collective spirit that define
the Yishun Secondary community. It stands as both a record-setting moment
and a lasting testament to what can be achieved when the community comes
together.</p>
<p>The event was a meaningful highlight of the YSS40 celebrations, bringing
the school community together to honour its shared past while looking ahead
with confidence to the future.</p>
