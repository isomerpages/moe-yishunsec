<h3><strong>YSS40 “Weave of Unity” Sets Singapore Book of Records</strong></h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Announcements/Weave_of_Unity_YSS40.jpg">
</div>
<p>As part of our YSS40 celebrations, Yishun Secondary School marked a special
milestone on 5 March 2026 with our “Weave of Unity” event. Students, teachers
and staff came together in a meaningful activity that symbolised the strength
of our community — each strand contributing to a stronger whole.</p>
<p>During the event, the YSS community successfully set a Singapore Book
of Records achievement for the Most Number of People Weaving Paracord Keychains
at the Same Time, with 1,151 participants taking part.</p>
<p>The paracord keychain serves as a tangible representation of the school’s
40-year journey and achievements. Each knot in the weave symbolises significant
milestones, successful cohorts, and notable achievements in the school’s
history. The vibrant colours of the paracord reflect the diverse talents
and successes of our students, staff and alumni.</p>
<p>The event was a meaningful highlight of our YSS40 celebrations, bringing
the school community together to celebrate our shared past while looking
ahead to the future.</p>
