<h3>Programme Highlights</h3>
<p>As AI becomes part of everyday life, Yishun Secondary School partnered
with DBS to run the <strong>Responsible AI Gameplay Programme</strong> for
all Secondary 2 students, held over two sessions on 29 June and 20 July
2026.</p>
<p>Working in teams, students took part in game-based challenges covering
deepfakes, AI-generated content, misinformation and scams, and learnt to
apply the SURE framework to evaluate information before trusting or sharing
it. The hands-on format encouraged collaboration and critical thinking,
going beyond a typical classroom lesson.</p>
<p>The second session was graced by <strong>Ms Goh Hanyan</strong>, Senior
Parliamentary Secretary (MCCY &amp; MSE), along with representatives from <strong>DBS, NCS, Cisco ASEAN </strong>and <strong>Google Cloud Singapore</strong>,
who interacted with students and saw the programme in action.</p>
<p>Students found the activities engaging and relevant, particularly the
deepfake and AI-photo challenges, and left with a clearer sense of how
to verify information responsibly.</p>
